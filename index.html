About

I work on graph deep learning. My research focuses on building neural network models that operate on graph-structured data — learning representations of nodes, edges, and whole graphs in ways that respect their underlying combinatorial structure. I'm broadly interested in the foundations of graph neural networks (GNNs), the mathematics behind geometric deep learning, and how these methods transfer to scientific and real-world problems. While theortical research is my passion, I care about doing useful, applied research.

I have tremendous experience building and managing enterprise systems. I'm experienced with both on-prem and AWS cloud systems. I hold 10 AWS certifications. I understand the importance of automation which provides velocity to development while improving quality. I understand the importance of cost-savings while maintaining a fault-tolerant architecture. I have held every position from developer to director to entrepreneur, which makes me uniquely positioned to architect a end-to-end AI system using the best possible techniques.
